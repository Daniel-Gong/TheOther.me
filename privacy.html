Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

At TheOther.me, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our Digital Self platform.

1. Introduction

TheOther.me ("we," "our," or "us") is an innovative technology company focused on Human-Centered AI. We build evolvable, predictable, and connectable Digital Selves for our users. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle your personal information in connection with our services, including our website, mobile applications, and platform features.

2. Information We Collect

To create and maintain your Digital Self, we collect various types of information that you authorize us to access:

2.1 Physical World Data

  • Health metrics from connected apps (e.g., Apple Health, Google Fit) including steps, heart rate, sleep patterns, and workout data
  • Physiological data that you choose to share

2.2 Device Interaction Data

  • Screen time and app usage patterns on your smartphones and computers
  • Device usage statistics and interaction patterns

2.3 Personal Information Data

  • Documents, photos, videos, notes, reminders, and calendar entries that you authorize us to access
  • Account information including email address and profile details

2.4 Digital Footprint Data

  • Browser history and web activity (with your explicit permission)
  • Social media activity and interactions (when connected)
  • Digital communication patterns

2.5 Technical Information

  • Device information (device type, operating system, unique device identifiers)
  • Log data (IP address, access times, pages viewed)
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

3.1 Core Platform Functions

  • Build Your Digital Self: Create and maintain a dynamic neural network model that reflects your behavior patterns, knowledge structures, and personal traits
  • Provide Insights: Generate visualizations of your time allocation, emotional cycles, health trends, and knowledge base
  • Enable Predictive Features: Power our FutureSelf™ and RealitySim™ features to predict future possibilities and simulate life trajectories

3.2 Intelligent Features

  • Trajectory Keeper: Monitor your behavior patterns and send alerts when you deviate from your stated goals
  • Knowledge Gap Finder: Analyze your knowledge base to identify learning opportunities and suggest resources
  • Personalization: Customize your experience and provide relevant recommendations

3.3 Social Features

  • Life Flow Sharing: Enable you to selectively share life fragments or growth trajectories with friends or communities (only with your explicit permission)
  • Community Connection: Facilitate authentic connections based on shared interests and growth patterns

3.4 Service Improvement

  • Improve and optimize our platform and services
  • Develop new features and functionality
  • Conduct research and analytics (using aggregated, anonymized data)
  • Provide customer support and respond to your inquiries

4. Data Processing and AI Models

Your data is used to train and maintain a dedicated neural network model that is unique to you. This model:

  • Continuously learns and evolves as more data is input
  • Becomes an increasingly accurate reflection of your intrinsic traits and extrinsic behaviors
  • Is stored securely and is only accessible to you (unless you choose to share specific insights)
  • Is not used to train general-purpose AI models that benefit other users

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We respect your privacy and do not sell your personal information. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

5.1 With Your Consent

  • When you explicitly choose to share "Life Flow" data or growth trajectories with friends or communities
  • When you authorize third-party integrations

5.2 Service Providers

  • With trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our platform (e.g., cloud storage, analytics, customer support)
  • These providers are contractually obligated to protect your information and use it only for specified purposes

5.3 Legal Requirements

  • When required by law, court order, or governmental regulation
  • To protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of our users
  • In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (with notice to users)

6. Data Security

We implement enterprise-grade security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit and at rest using industry-standard encryption protocols
  • Access Controls: Strict access controls and authentication mechanisms
  • Regular Audits: Ongoing security assessments and vulnerability testing
  • Data Minimization: We only collect and retain data necessary for providing our services

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

7.1 Access and Portability

  • Access your personal data and Digital Self model
  • Export your data in a machine-readable format

7.2 Correction and Updates

  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Update your profile and preferences

7.3 Deletion

  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Remove specific data sources or integrations

7.4 Control and Consent

  • Revoke permissions for data sources at any time
  • Control what information is shared in your "Life Flow"
  • Opt-out of certain data processing activities (subject to service limitations)

7.5 Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide our services. When you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal information within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it by law.

8. Children's Privacy

TheOther.me is not intended for children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of consent in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. These countries may have data protection laws that differ from those in your country. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

10. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Analyze how you use our platform
  • Provide personalized content and features

You can control cookies through your browser settings, though this may affect certain features of our platform.

11. Third-Party Services

Our platform may integrate with third-party services (e.g., Apple Health, Google Fit, social media platforms). These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party services.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of material changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy on our website
  • Sending you an email notification (if you have an account)
  • Displaying a prominent notice on our platform

Your continued use of our services after such changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

13. Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@theother.me
  • Company: TheOther Intelligence LLC

Your privacy is fundamental to our mission of Human-Centered AI. We are committed to transparency, security, and giving you control over your Digital Self.
