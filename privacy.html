Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

Oria AI is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app and services.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy applies to Oria AI ("we," "our," or "us"), operated by TheOther Intelligence LLC. It describes how we handle your personal information when you use our website, mobile app, and related services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you choose to provide or connect, which may include:

  • Account information (e.g., email)
  • Messages and content you enter in the app (e.g., conversations with Oria AI)
  • Data from services you connect with your permission (e.g., calendar events when you connect Google Calendar)
  • Usage and device information necessary to operate and improve our services

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to provide, personalize, and improve Oria AI—including powering the features you use within the app. This includes sending certain data to third-party AI services to generate responses and insights (see Section 5 below). We do not sell your personal information.

4. Google User Data (Calendar)

When you connect your Google account, we request access only to your Google Calendar data. We use the following scopes:

  • calendar.readonly — to view your calendar events
  • calendar.events — to create, edit, and manage calendar events on your behalf

We use Google user data only to provide or improve the calendar features within the Oria AI app. We do not use Google user data for advertising, analytics, selling, or any purpose other than providing your calendar viewing and event management functionality. We comply with the Google API Services User Data Policy.

5. Third-Party AI Services

Oria AI uses third-party large language model (LLM) providers to power its AI features. Before any personal data is sent to these services, the app will ask for your explicit consent.

5.1 Who We Send Data To

We may transmit data to the following third-party AI service providers:

  • OpenAI (OpenAI, L.L.C.) — Privacy Policy
  • Anthropic (Anthropic, PBC) — Privacy Policy
  • xAI (xAI Corp.) — Privacy Policy
  • Google (Google LLC) — Privacy Policy

5.2 What Data Is Sent

When you interact with Oria AI's features, the following types of data may be sent to the third-party AI providers listed above:

  • Messages and text you enter in conversations with Oria AI
  • Contextual data from connected services that you have authorized (e.g., calendar event details if you connected Google Calendar)
  • Profile or preference information you provide within the app to personalize AI responses

We do not send your email address, password, payment information, or device identifiers to these AI providers.

5.3 How Data Is Used by Third-Party AI Providers

Data sent to these providers is used solely to generate AI-powered responses and insights within the Oria AI app. We access these services through their API platforms, which are governed by their respective data processing agreements. Under these agreements, the providers do not use your data to train or improve their models.

5.4 Your Consent

The app will clearly inform you and request your permission before any personal data is shared with third-party AI services. You may withdraw your consent at any time through the app's settings, though this may limit the availability of certain AI-powered features.

5.5 Protections

All data transmitted to third-party AI providers is encrypted in transit. We have reviewed the privacy practices of each provider and confirm that they provide protections for user data consistent with or equivalent to those described in this Privacy Policy. We use API-level access with data processing terms that restrict providers from using your data beyond fulfilling our requests.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We may share information only: (1) with your consent, (2) with service providers (including the third-party AI providers described in Section 5) who help us operate our services under strict confidentiality, or (3) when required by law or to protect rights and safety.

7. Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your data. No transmission or storage is 100% secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Your Rights

You can access, correct, or delete your data and control connected sources through the app or by contacting us. We will respond to requests in accordance with applicable law.

9. Children

Our services are not intended for users under 13 (or the applicable age in your region). We do not knowingly collect data from children.

10. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. We will post the updated policy on our website and, where appropriate, notify you. Continued use after changes means you accept the updated policy.

11. Contact

For privacy questions or requests:

  • Email: privacy@theother.me
  • Company: TheOther Intelligence LLC
