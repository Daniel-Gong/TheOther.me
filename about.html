About Us

TheOther Intelligence LLC · Oria AI

We build Oria AI—an evolvable personal AI that helps people turn fragmented life data into clear, actionable wisdom—while respecting privacy, consent, and human agency.

Who we are

Oria AI is developed and operated by TheOther Intelligence LLC, a company focused on personal AI that integrates health, productivity, memory, and communication signals with explicit user control. Our public product home and domain for the app experience is oria.me.

What we believe

Modern life generates enormous amounts of digital signal, but that information usually sits in separate apps and devices. We believe the next step for personal technology is not more dashboards—it is synthesis: connecting what you choose to share, surfacing patterns early, and supporting intentional decisions without replacing professional care or judgment.

We write about these themes on our blog and design the product around transparent data practices; see our Privacy Policy for how we handle information.

Ecosystem & support

Oria AI’s work is informed by collaboration across the innovation community. Our homepage recognizes support from the Rothberg Build Fund, Yale Ventures, and the Yale Center for Biomedical Innovation and Technology (CBIT).
